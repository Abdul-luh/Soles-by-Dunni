The Story

Crafted with Soul

Soles by Dunni was born from a love of beautiful, intentional footwear. Every pair tells a story — of craftsmanship, care, and the belief that what you wear on your feet shapes how you move through the world.

Based in Lagos, we create sandals and loafers that pair effortlessly with your lifestyle. From the streets of Lagos Island to your most cherished moments, Dunni's soles carry you.

Delivery Information

Custom Crafted (Made to Order)

Since most of our footwear is made to order, please allow 7–10 working days for production and quality checks before dispatch.

We ship to Abuja, Port Harcourt, Ibadan and all other states. 5–7 working days after production.
